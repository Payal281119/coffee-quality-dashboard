# ☕ Coffee Quality Dashboard: Global Arabica Insights

## 📊 Project Overview
A dynamic and interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze **CQI Arabica coffee quality data**.  
The project explores sensory attributes, processing methods, defects, and geographic trends to understand their impact on **Total Cup Points**.

This dashboard enables users to uncover quality drivers, compare coffee origins, and gain data-driven insights into global specialty coffee performance.

---

## 📌 Purpose

This dashboard analyzes ~200 specialty Arabica coffee samples across 9 key sensory attributes such as Aroma, Acidity, Flavor, Body, and Balance.
It helps coffee producers, quality analysts, and data enthusiasts uncover patterns that influence overall coffee quality and scoring.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development & visualization  
- 🔄 **Power Query** – Data cleaning & transformation  
- 🧠 **DAX** – Calculated measures & dynamic analysis  
- 📑 **Microsoft Excel** – Initial data preparation & EDA  
- 🗂️ **GitHub** – Version control & project sharing  
- 📁 File Formats – `.pbix` (development), `.png` (preview)

---

## 📂 Data Source

Dataset includes ~200 Arabica coffee samples (2010–2018 harvest years) stored in a flat table.

Key fields:
- Total Cup Points  
- Aroma, Acidity, Flavor, Aftertaste, Body, Balance  
- Processing Method  
- Country & Region  
- Variety  
- Altitude  
- Category One & Two Defects  

---

## ⭐ Features & Highlights

### 🔎 Business Problem
Specialty coffee stakeholders need to identify which sensory attributes, processing methods, and defect patterns most influence **Total Cup Points** to improve quality and competitiveness.

---

### ❓ Key Questions Answered

- Which processing method scores highest?
- Does harvest year affect quality?
- Which sensory attributes drive Total Cup Points?
- Which countries produce the highest average scores?

---

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
To visually explore coffee quality patterns and reveal relationships between sensory scores, defects, origin, and Total Cup Points.

---

## 📊 Key Visuals (Brief Walkthrough)

### 🏠 Overview Page
- KPI Cards – Avg Total Cup Points, Total Defects, Countries, Top Processing Method  
- Scatter Plots – Flavor, Aftertaste & Acidity vs Total Cup Points  
- Map – Country-wise performance  
- Sensory Bar Chart – Attribute comparison  

### 📈 Total Cup Points Analysis
- Breakdown by Country, Region, Variety, Processing Method & Harvest Year  
- Altitude Analysis – Higher altitude often correlates with better scores  

### 🌸 Sensory Attributes Page
- Grouped bar charts comparing Sweetness, Flavor & Aroma  
- Pattern discovery across origins and methods  

### ⚠️ Defects Page
- Category One & Two defect summary  
- Country-wise defect comparison  
- Decomposition Tree to track score drivers  

---

## 💡 Business Impact & Insights

- 📈 **Quality Optimization** – Identify strongest sensory drivers of high scores  
- 🌍 **Market Positioning** – Highlight top-performing origins & methods  
- ⚠️ **Defect Reduction** – Spot defect-heavy regions for improvement  
- 📊 **Strategic Sourcing** – Help buyers select high-quality harvests
